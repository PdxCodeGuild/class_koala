Bio Page for Scorpion

Welcome to Scorpions Bio Page


Scorpion is a fictional character from the Mortal Kombat game franchise.He is one of the more unique characters as he is both a ninja and a necromancer, He was aproud member of a distinguished japanese ninja clan and lived a fairly peacful life. Scorpion has a pretty tumultuous life especially after his family is murdered by a rival ninja clan, he has made it his lifes mission to get revenge for his family and will stop at nothing to quench his thirst for vengeance. As you learn more and more about him and his history through the varies version of mortal kombat you why he is so driven in his quest for revenge.
